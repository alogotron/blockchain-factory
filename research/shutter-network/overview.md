## What Is Shutter Network?

Shutter Network is a **threshold encryption protocol** for Ethereum designed to prevent **front-running and MEV (Maximal Extractable Value)** by encrypting transactions before they're included in a block. Transactions are decrypted only after inclusion, eliminating the ability for validators or searchers to reorder them for profit.

**Core components:**
- **Keypers** — decentralized nodes that collectively generate encryption/decryption keys using distributed key generation (DKG)
- **Shutter API** — for dApp developers to integrate threshold encryption into their apps
- **SHU token** — governance token for Shutter DAO 0x36
- **EIP work** — Encrypted Mempool EIP, promoting onchain censorship resistance
