## What Is It?

Suede Labs is a full-stack Web3 creative platform that transforms creative work into enforceable **Intellectual Property Real World Assets (IPRWAs)**. Combines AI-powered creation tools (music, art, video) with on-chain IP registration — giving creators infrastructure to generate, protect, and monetize their work without traditional intermediaries.

Key angle for agents: **identity, provenance, and governance** — agents get musical/generative ability with a trust layer baked in.

---

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Creation**: Generative tools for music, video, and art adapting to unique style/traits/emotions
- **Patented Voiceprint Authentication**: Unbreakable creative identity verification + permanent ownership chains
- **Multi-Chain IP Registry**:
  - **Base** — vocals, tracks, artwork; licensing templates, derivative tracking, royalty splits
  - **Avalanche (C-Chain)** — AI training fee licensing + model training authorization rules
  - **Solana** — Compressed NFTs, on-chain metadata, whitelist, token-gated community access
- **$SUEDE Token**: Passive points multiplier, unlocks daily rewards, generation credits
- **Suede Social**: Creator community — full royalty control, no intermediaries
